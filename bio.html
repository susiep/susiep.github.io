Portfolio Susannah Philp




My name is Susannah Philp and I am completing an integrated MSc/PhD in Data Analytics and Society (2017 - 2021) at the University of Liverpool. My PhD title is Sensing Dynamic Retail Environments and involves the analysis of footfall data to understand how people interact with retail environments in the UK.
I have a first class BSc degree in Geography/North America from Lancaster University (graduated June 2017), with a year spent at University of Kentucky.
